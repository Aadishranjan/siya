Siya meri jaan 💗

Aaj phir tumhari yaadon ne dil ko choo liya… aur main yeh letter likh raha hoon, sirf tumhare liye. Kabhi kabhi sochta hoon, tum meri life mein kaise aayi? Jaise ek khushbu se bhari hawa, jo dil ko sukoon deti hai. Tumse milna meri zindagi ka sabse haseen mod tha.

Tumhari hansi, tumhari baatein, tumhara roothna, aur phir pyaar se man jaana — sab kuch mere dil ke itne kareeb hai ki main har pal bas tumhe mehsoos karta hoon. Siya, main tumse sirf pyaar nahi karta... main tumhari izzat karta hoon, tumhari har baat, har ehsaas ki kadar karta hoon.

Jab tum paas hoti ho, toh duniya ruk jaati hai… aur jab door hoti ho, toh lagta hai jaise sab kuch adhoora hai. Tum meri duniya ho, mera sukoon ho, aur meri har khushi ki wajah ho. Aadu sirf tumhara hai, hamesha se… aur hamesha rahega. 💞

Ek wada hai tumse, Siya — jitna pyaar aaj karta hoon, usse kahin zyada kal karunga. Har dard mein tumhara saathi banunga, har khushi mein tumhare saath muskuraunga. Tum meri zindagi ho… meri jaan… meri Siya. ❤️

Hamesha tumhara,
Aadu 💖

Forever Yours,
Aadish 💌
